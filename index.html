3) PPG INDUSTRIES: work experience

My latest work experience, pride and joy was at PPG Industries, a leading global company in the manufacturing of paint. My task was to revamp their main website (https://www.ppg.com/en-US) amd other sister websites base on mockup provided by the graphic designers. Anything in between the navigation bar to the footer, I had my hands on it. I added animation whenever a user is scrolling down the page, mouse hover effect on cards, dropdown menu, accordion that show their content or not etc. I truly felt value for my contribution when I was working with them unlike the first time at Ricardo Cuisine which added fuel to my curiosity about web design in general.
